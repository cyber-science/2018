Camera-Ready Submission

Please read the following notice before uploading your paper (for CyberSciTech, PICom, DASC, DataCom, and all associated workshops/special sessions).

1. The page limits are:
Regular Papers: 8 + 2 pages for purchase, 50 USD per extra page
WiP/Special Session/Workshop Papers: 6 + 2 pages for purchase, 50 USD per extra page
Poster Papers: 4 + 2 pages for purchase, 50 USD per extra page

Camera ready submissions must follow the following template format:
https://www.computer.org/cms/CPS/app/8x11-2/instruct8.5x11x2.doc
https://www.computer.org/cms/CPS/app/8x11-2/IEEECS_confs_LaTeX.zip

2. The final upload deadline is 30 June 2018. The final paper submission link is here:
https://ieeecps.org/#!/auth/login?ak=1&pid=4vlZj0qOv46TFCW2R3FHQh
All paper registrations must be done by 27 June 2018.

If you have any questions, please e-mail them to cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com, with the full author names and paper ID (the 10 digital ID number given by EDAS). During preparation of your final camera-ready manuscript, any change to the list of authors and paper title must be informed with valid reasons by emailing cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com and must be approved by the conference chairs.

Registration

Alongisde submission of your paper, please make sure to register for the conference.

Invitation Letter

If you need an invitation letter, please email Prof. Klimmis Ntalianis at kdal75@gmail.com (cc cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com) after you have completed registration with your name, institution affiliation, paper ID, paper title, and registration confirmation to request for an invitation letter.
